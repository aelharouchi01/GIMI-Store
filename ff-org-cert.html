Overview

Build foresight into how you grow.

The Future Foresight Organizational Certification recognizes organizations with structured capabilities to sustain growth through foresight and scenarios. It assesses organizational maturity using the S4Futures framework, emphasizing evidence-based future foresight with uncertainty at its core.

It complements the Organization Innovation Certification, certifying how well your organization anticipates change and turns it into strategy.

What We Evaluate

The four S4Futures pillars.

Scoping & Designing

Framing the foresight challenge and designing the approach.

Scanning & Screening

Detecting trends, weak signals, and drivers of change.

Scenarios & Foresight

Building rigorous, evidence-based scenarios under uncertainty.

Strategy & Innovation

Turning foresight into strategy and innovation decisions.

Maturity Levels

Certified across four levels.

Management · Proficient

Foundational phase with partial scenario integration.

Management · Master

Clear internal responsibilities and strategic analysis of change drivers.

Leadership · Proficient

Advanced integration linking metrics with foresight projects.

Leadership · Master

Dedicated foresight department with senior leadership involvement.

How It Works

A four-step certification path.

1 · Initiation

Initial contact and calendar agreement.

2 · Submission & Analysis

Documentation review and stakeholder interviews.

3 · Report & Evaluation

Auditor assessment and maturity scoring.

4 · Certification

Official award and certificate issuance.
