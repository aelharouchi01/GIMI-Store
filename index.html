About GIMI

The world's largest organization for innovation professionals.

Founded in 2009 by a worldwide group of chief innovation officers, executives, academics and consultants, GIMI exists to make innovation a professional, measurable, certifiable business discipline.

In 2023 GIMI joined forces with the International Association of Innovation Professionals (IAOIP), forming the largest professional organization for innovation in the world.

2023 · Joining forces with IAOIP

IAOIP brought a worldwide community of practitioners, the International Journal of Innovation Science, and deep involvement in ISO standards development, uniting rigorous accredited certification with an active global community.

About GIMI

Making innovation a management discipline.

The Global Innovation Management Institute is a global, nonprofit organization and the worldwide standard certification body for innovation and innovation management.

Who We Are

Why we exist, and where we come from.

Mission

Democratize innovation

To democratize innovation worldwide by certifying one million individuals and companies, and spreading the adoption of innovation standards and best practices.

Vision

A professional discipline

To make innovation a professional business discipline taught in academic institutions, corporate and government academies, and adopted across organizations.

History

Founded in 2009

Born from the thought leaders of innovation practice: pioneers from firms like Arthur D. Little, Monitor Group and Parthenon, together with academia including Harvard. In 2023, GIMI's acquisition of the IAOIP added a strong academic angle and made it the largest global innovation certification body.

Why GIMI

The certifying body and partner of choice.

Eight reasons organizations and professionals choose GIMI to make innovation a measurable, repeatable discipline.

01 · Authority

Standards leadership and institutional authority

What makes GIMI structurally unique in the global system.

Only body globally accredited to ISO/IEC 17024.
  • Lead driver of the ISO 56000 series through leadership and advisory roles
  • Licensed by ANSI to sell ISO standards
  • Methodology independently audited and endorsed as ISO 56002+
  • Leadership includes the founders of the US ISO Technical Advisory Group for Innovation
02 · Methodology

Proprietary capability system (ISO+)

How GIMI delivers innovation, beyond standards.

Three pillars: Strategy, Capacity, Discipline.
  • The High-Performance Innovation (HPI) framework, a structured, actionable system
  • A common language for innovation across organizations
  • Goes beyond compliance into execution (ISO+)
  • Makes innovation repeatable, scalable and measurable
03 · Execution

Execution model and learning experience

How GIMI ensures real-world outcomes, not just knowledge.

"Show and Tell": learners ship real outputs.
  • Action-oriented pedagogy based on the "do vs. learn" principle
  • Tangible outputs such as prototypes and business cases
  • Education 4.0 approach: micro-learning, gamification and problem-based learning
  • Focus on real business challenges and implementation
04 · Ecosystem

Product ecosystem and scalable programs

What GIMI offers across segments, and how it scales.

From youth (GIMI Impact) to C-suite (CCIO).
  • Turnkey programs designed for immediate deployment
  • Adaptable to corporations, governments, SMEs and universities
  • Proven at scale through the 10X Program (Colombia) and Innovation Olympics
05 · Technology

Technology and AI-driven infrastructure

The digital layer enabling speed and scale.

AI-powered: Akaio and Eureka Online.
  • Akaio explores hidden trends, future scenarios and partner matching
  • Eureka Online runs the end-to-end innovation workflow
  • Automated diagnostics: the Innovation Potential Assessment and Innovation Mindset Index
  • Faster, cheaper, more scalable innovation execution
06 · Reach

Global ecosystem, reach and partnerships

The scale of influence and network effects.

18,000+ professionals in 100+ countries.
  • 1,600+ corporations worldwide
  • Partnerships with governments (Colombia, UAE, Brazil), corporations (NASA, P&G, IBM) and universities, including Ivy League
  • 50+ strategic partners and a global training network
  • Collaboration with Emerald Publishing for the International Journal of Innovation Science
07 · Knowledge

Thought leadership and knowledge platforms

How GIMI shapes the field intellectually.

Publisher of the Journal of Innovation Science.
  • A global think tank and speaker bureau of innovation leaders
  • Continuous research and content in innovation management
  • Helps shape global innovation discourse and standards
08 · Moat

Market position and competitive moat

Why GIMI is difficult to replicate.

Combines ISO authority, tools and global scale.
  • The largest and most established innovation certification ecosystem globally
  • No open-market competitor with comparable tools, frameworks and certification credibility
  • Strong first-mover advantage and institutional legitimacy

GIMI members gain priority publishing and, at the academic level, faculty Associate Editor opportunities, making the Journal both a research resource and a platform to share your own thought leadership with a global audience.
