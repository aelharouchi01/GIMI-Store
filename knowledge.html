International Journal of Innovation Science

Peer-reviewed research on the science of innovation.

The International Journal of Innovation Science is a peer-reviewed publication compiling work from innovation experts on the developments and applications of innovation science. It came to GIMI through the 2023 merger with IAOIP and is published in collaboration with Emerald Publishing.

GIMI members gain priority publishing and, at the academic level, faculty Associate Editor opportunities, making the Journal both a research resource and a platform to share your own thought leadership with a global audience.
