International Journal of Innovation Science

A global, peer-reviewed authority on innovation science.

Published since 2007, IJIS is the official peer-reviewed journal of GIMI and the International Association of Innovation Professionals (IAOIP), produced in collaboration with Emerald Publishing: a global forum for advanced knowledge in innovation.

Aims & Scope

Advanced knowledge in innovation: emerging technologies, best practices, tools, metrics, organizational design, change management, and leadership.

Double-Anonymous Peer Review

Every manuscript is evaluated under a rigorous double-anonymous review process.

Bi-Monthly

Six issues a year of fundamental and applied innovation research, read worldwide.

Publish With IJIS

Members gain priority publishing and, for academics, faculty Associate Editor opportunities.
