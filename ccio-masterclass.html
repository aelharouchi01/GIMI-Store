Overview

Lead innovation across the enterprise.

An executive cohort plus guided mentoring that equips senior leaders to build and run an innovation management system, earning CCIO Level 3 and Level 4.

The Certified Chief Innovation Officer (CCIO) pathway is GIMI's executive certification for building and leading innovation management systems. Through a live masterclass and a guided mentoring program, leaders learn to make innovation repeatable across the organization using GIMI's SCDP framework (Strategy, Capacity, Discipline, Performance). The Masterclass delivers both levels in one cohort.

Curriculum

Step 1: Certified Innovation Management (Level 3).

The masterclass builds the four core capabilities of a chief innovation officer, then Level 4 applies them to a real project in your organization.

I · Strategy for Innovation & Growth

Define a Case for Change that gives innovation purpose, identify strategic spaces to focus on, and develop your innovation portfolio.

II · Capacity to Drive Results

Identify leaders to manage innovation, improve the process from idea to commercialization, and map partners across the value chain.

III · Discipline to Follow Through

Drive an innovation mindset, use metrics to guide behaviors and results, and agree the strategic next steps to improve.

IV · World-Class Performance

Define how value is created, assess and ensure value capture, and leverage AI for cheaper, faster, bolder solutions.

Step 2 (Certified Innovation Leader, Level 4) applies the Innovation System Framework to a real project: you build and improve an innovation management system for your organization, then document a case study (up to three months to submit) for expert review and certification.
