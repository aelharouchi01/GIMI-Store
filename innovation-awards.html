Thought Leadership · Innovation Awards

Recognizing the world's boldest innovators.

The GIMI Innovation Awards recognize and celebrate the best organizations and individuals driving innovation across the public, private, and social sectors. Winners gain global recognition, credibility, media exposure, and a place in GIMI's worldwide network of innovators.

About the Awards

Innovation that is judged, not just claimed.

Every entry is scored against published criteria by an expert jury, through application and review, virtual pitches, and a global awards ceremony with keynotes and panels. GIMI members can apply for free; non-members can purchase event tickets.
