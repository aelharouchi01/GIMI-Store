Overview

Five teams. Eight weeks. Your next strategic move.

The Innovation Olympics is an open innovation competition for leaders looking for their next strategic growth opportunity, without committing years or a large internal team to find it.

Rather than a single consulting team, GIMI mobilizes five teams in parallel, each guided by experienced mentors and drawn from a global network of 150+ universities. Over an accelerated eight-week sprint they explore your challenge from multiple angles, applying a structured, proven methodology to move from raw ideas to fields-of-play, business concepts, and preliminary business cases, with guidance from your key stakeholders throughout. The approach has generated strategic growth opportunities for more than 100 global companies.
