Overview

From innovation challenge to real business results.

When you need outcomes, not just training, the accelerator (also known as the EPR or IXLerator) drives a portfolio of projects to implementation while building lasting capability inside your teams.

Over 10 to 12 weeks, a minimum of five teams inside your organization work in parallel through GIMI's proven methodology, supported by workshops, coaching, and the Eureka platform. Teams move from idea generation to a pipeline of innovation concepts and prototypes, so you finish with both tangible results and people certified to keep delivering them.
