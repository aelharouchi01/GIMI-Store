Overview

Prove your innovation maturity.

A prestigious credential that benchmarks how systematically your organization manages and leads innovation, against world-class standards.

The GIMI Organization Certification certifies the maturity of an organization's innovation practices and aligns them with world-class standards. It assesses your innovation management system using GIMI's High-Performance Innovation (HPI) framework, which has been independently audited and endorsed as ISO+ compliant and aligned to ISO 56001.

Following the assessment, certified organizations receive a prestigious certificate and a letter of award issued directly by GIMI, plus access to a global community of innovation experts.

At a glance

  • Certified at Levels 1 to 4 of maturity
  • Built on the HPI framework: Strategy, Capacity, Discipline
  • ISO+ compliant, aligned to ISO 56001
  • Certificate and letter of award from GIMI
  • Access to a global community of experts
Best for organizations ready to benchmark and signal their innovation maturity.

What We Evaluate

Three pillars of high-performance innovation.

Strategy

A clear innovation vision, ambition, and strategy linked to measurable goals.

Capacity

The people, processes, alliances, and resources that make innovation happen consistently.

Discipline

The governance, metrics, and culture that make innovation repeatable and measurable.

Maturity Levels

A climb from Level 1 to Level 4.

Certification recognizes how far your innovation system has matured, from defined management practices to ecosystem leadership.

1
Management
Proficient
Defined processes, alliances, and strategy-linked metrics.
2
Management
Master
Innovation communicated across timeframes, with trained teams and formal recognition.
3
Leadership
Proficient
Clear targets, multi-horizon portfolios, systematic resource allocation, and formal KPIs.
4
Leadership
Master
Recognized ecosystem leaders with remarkable results and dynamic processes.

How It Works

From pre-audit to award.

1
Pre-audit
A pre-audit assessment identifies your current maturity level.
2
Select tier
Choose the appropriate certification tier based on the assessment.
3
Audit
Expert-led audit against the HPI framework, aligned to ISO 56001.
4
Award
Receive your certificate and letter of award issued by GIMI.

Certification can be pursued via a self-audit (using GIMI's audit tool) or a third-party GIMI-certified auditor. The Organization Maturity Certification is $2,500 for each level attained.
