Overview

Prove your innovation maturity.

The GIMI Organization Certification showcases an organization's strong commitment to innovation by certifying the maturity level of its innovation practices and aligning them with world-class standards. It assesses your innovation management system using GIMI's High-Performance Innovation (HPI) framework, which has been independently audited and endorsed as ISO+ compliant.

Following the assessment, certified organizations receive a prestigious certificate and a letter of award issued directly by GIMI, plus access to a global community of innovation experts.

What We Evaluate

Three pillars of high-performance innovation.

Strategy

A clear innovation vision, ambition, and strategy linked to measurable goals.

Capacity

The people, processes, alliances, and resources that make innovation happen consistently.

Discipline

The governance, metrics, and culture that make innovation repeatable and measurable.

Maturity Levels

Certified at Levels 1 to 4.

Level 1 · Proficient

Innovation Management: defined processes, alliances, and strategy-linked metrics.

Level 2 · Master

Innovation Management: innovation communicated across timeframes, with trained teams and formal recognition.

Level 3 · Proficient

Innovation Leadership: clear targets, multi-horizon portfolios, systematic resource allocation, and formal KPIs.

Level 4 · Master

Innovation Leadership: recognized ecosystem leaders with remarkable results and dynamic processes.

How It Works

From pre-audit to award.

1 · Pre-audit

A pre-audit assessment identifies your current maturity level.

2 · Select tier

Choose the appropriate certification tier based on the assessment.

3 · Audit

Expert-led audit against the HPI framework, aligned to ISO 56001.

4 · Award

Receive your certificate and letter of award issued by GIMI.
