Overview

Become qualified to audit innovation management.

As ISO 56001 adoption grows, organizations need auditors who can assess innovation management systems with rigor and consistency.

The Certified GIMI Auditor program is a three-session online masterclass where you learn to conduct audits for the GIMI framework and ISO 56001. It is built for professionals who conduct or oversee audits of innovation management systems and who are responsible for aligning organizational practices with global innovation standards. You leave with an in-depth understanding of both frameworks and the practical tools to assess the innovation maturity of any organization.
