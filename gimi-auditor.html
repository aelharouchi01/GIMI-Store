Overview

Become qualified to audit innovation management.

As ISO 56001 adoption grows, organizations need auditors who can assess innovation management systems with rigor and consistency.

The Certified GIMI Auditor program prepares experienced professionals, consultants, and internal auditors to evaluate an organization's innovation management system against ISO 56001. You learn the requirements of the standard, a structured audit methodology, and how to gather evidence, score maturity, and guide organizations toward certification.

What You Learn

Everything you need to run an audit.

The ISO 56001 Requirements

A working command of the innovation-management-system standard and the wider ISO 56000 family.

Audit Methodology

How to plan and run a structured, defensible audit using the GIMI framework.

Evidence & Scoring

How to gather evidence and score maturity objectively across Strategy, Capacity, and Discipline.

Reporting & Guidance

How to report findings and guide organizations toward certification.
