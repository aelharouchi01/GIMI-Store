Overview

Get your leadership team pointing the same way.

Innovation stalls when leaders disagree on where to play. These workshops create the alignment to move, fast.

GIMI facilitators run an audit upfront to understand what your organization already has, conduct primary research with key leaders, then guide the leadership team through a structured workshop. Together you identify key growth targets, prioritize the fields-of-play worth pursuing, and agree on where to focus innovation efforts across the organization, leaving with a shared roadmap rather than a list of opinions.
