Overview

From AI hype to a working agent.

Most teams are still debating what AI could do. This program has you build something that does it.

The Certified AI Agent Practitioner program teaches you to operationalize AI through GIMI's Innovation 5.0 approach, moving from a real use case to a working AI agent you can put to work in the innovation process.
