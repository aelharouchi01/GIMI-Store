Overview

Turn innovation from luck into a managed system.

Most organizations innovate in bursts. This certification gives leaders the framework to make it repeatable, measurable, and certifiable, in full alignment with the ISO 56000 family of standards.

The Certified ISO Innovation Management Expert (CIME) is built for leaders who want to establish, implement, and improve an Innovation Management System (IMS) their whole organization can rely on. You learn to design the strategy, structures, and metrics that move a company beyond sporadic creativity toward disciplined, measurable performance, then anchor it to ISO 56001, the world's first certifiable innovation-management standard.

It is the bridge between GIMI's High-Performance Innovation methodology and the international ISO 56000 standards, taught by experts who help write those standards.

The ISO 56000 Family

The standards you'll work across.

The program builds fluency across the ISO 56000 family of innovation-management standards, with ISO 56001 — the certifiable management-system requirements — at its core.

ISO 56000

Fundamentals and vocabulary for innovation management.

ISO 56001

Innovation management system — requirements (the certifiable standard).

ISO 56002

Innovation management system — guidance for implementation.

ISO 56003

Tools and methods for innovation partnership.

ISO/TR 56004

Innovation management assessment.

ISO 56005

Tools and methods for intellectual property management.

ISO 56006

Tools and methods for strategic intelligence management.

ISO 56007

Tools and methods for idea management.

ISO 56008

Tools and methods for innovation operation measurements.
