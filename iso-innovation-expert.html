Overview

Systematize innovation to the global standard.

The ISO Innovation Management Expert certification is designed for leaders who want to establish, implement, and improve Innovation Management Systems (IMS) in full alignment with the ISO 56000 family. Participants gain a complete framework to design and systematize innovation management, moving beyond sporadic creativity to disciplined, measurable performance.

It is the bridge between GIMI's High-Performance Innovation methodology and the international ISO 56000 standards, taught by experts who contribute to the standards themselves.
