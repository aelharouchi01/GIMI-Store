Why the Think Tank Exists

Where innovation standards meet real-world execution.

Innovation is widely discussed and rarely executed systematically. The Think Tank closes that gap, bringing global leaders together every week to share measurable, applicable insights grounded in the ISO 56000 standards for innovation management.
