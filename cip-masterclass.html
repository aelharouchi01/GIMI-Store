Overview

Master the innovation process, end to end.

One guided, expert-led cohort that takes you from learning the innovation toolkit to applying it on a real growth challenge, earning CIP Level 1 and Level 2.

The Certified Innovation Professional (CIP) pathway is GIMI's core innovation-process certification: it teaches you to generate breakthrough ideas and turn them into real, commercialized outcomes for your organization. The Masterclass delivers both levels in one guided, expert-led cohort, moving you from learning the tools to applying them on a live challenge.

Level 1 · Associate

Curriculum

Level 1: six modules over eight weeks.

The Level 1 (Associate) course walks you through the full innovation process, one module at a time, building the toolkit you apply in Level 2.

1 · Demystifying Innovation

Learn what business innovation really is and gain the tools, techniques, and mindset to pursue it.

2 · Innovation Process & Intent

Agree where and where not to innovate, and set your growth targets and investment profile.

3 · Opportunity Insights

Explore future-shaping trends, identify insights and ideas, and organize them into an opportunity map.

4 · Fields of Play

Define new areas for the company to play, then prioritize and develop them into compelling opportunities.

5 · Business Concepts

Generate new business concepts and use a reverse-engineering tactic to validate them.

6 · Business Case

Summarize the concept compellingly and learn what it takes to sell a new idea internally.

Level 2 (Master) then applies this toolkit to a real-world challenge, generating new innovation opportunities for a partner organization and submitting documented work for expert review.
