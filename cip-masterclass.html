Overview

Master the innovation process, end to end.

One guided, expert-led cohort that takes you from learning the innovation toolkit to applying it on a real growth challenge, earning CIP Level 1 and Level 2.

The Certified Innovation Professional (CIP) pathway is GIMI's core innovation-process certification: it teaches you to generate breakthrough ideas and turn them into real, commercialized outcomes for your organization. The Masterclass delivers both levels in one guided, expert-led cohort, moving you from learning the tools to applying them on a live challenge.

Level 1 · Associate

Generate breakthrough ideas

Learn the end-to-end innovation process and the tools to create more and better ideas, then apply them to real opportunities in your organization.

What it covers
  • Innovation fundamentals, mindset, and language
  • The innovation process: Innovation Intent, Opportunity Insights, Fields of Play, Business Concepts, and Business Case
  • Idea-generation tools and techniques for breakthrough thinking
Format & materials
  • 8 × 3-hour live online sessions with experts
  • Online proctored exam · IMBOK Level 1 Guide (e-copy)
Level 2 · Master

Turn ideas into real outcomes

Apply your Level 1 toolkit to a real growth challenge, carrying an idea through to a tangible, commercializable outcome for your organization.

What it covers
  • Build a case study on new growth ideas or opportunities
  • Document your process, decisions, and learnings
  • Submit for expert evaluation and feedback
Format & materials
  • 4 weeks of mentoring with an innovation expert
  • Expert project review · Level 2 project guideline
