Overview

Build innovation capability, end to end.

The Certified Innovation Professional (CIP) program helps professionals build practical innovation knowledge, tools, and methodologies to drive innovation within their organizations. The Masterclass delivers both levels of the CIP pathway in one guided cohort:

Level 1 — Associate. Covers innovation fundamentals, tools, and the skills to develop effective innovation ideas and results.

Level 2 — Master. Focuses on applying those skills to solve a real-world challenge and create innovation opportunities for your organization.
