Overview

Manage innovation to the global standard.

ISO 56001 turns innovation from a set of activities into a managed system. This assessment shows how close you are, and what it takes to certify.

Built on GIMI's High-Performance Innovation framework and mapped to the ISO 56000 family, the assessment audits your innovation management system against the requirements of ISO 56001. It scores your maturity, identifies the gaps between where you are and a certifiable system, and delivers a roadmap to close them, so you can formalize, benchmark, and ultimately certify your innovation management.

What We Evaluate

The pillars of a managed innovation system.

Strategy

Innovation vision, intent, and leadership commitment, linked to measurable goals.

Capacity

The people, processes, resources, and partnerships that make innovation happen.

Discipline

The governance, metrics, and culture that make innovation repeatable and certifiable.
