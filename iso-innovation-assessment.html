Overview

Manage innovation to the global standard.

ISO 56001 turns innovation from a set of activities into a managed system, one you can define, run, measure, and certify.

The standard provides comprehensive guidelines to define an innovation strategy, manage innovation processes, foster an innovation culture, and enhance innovation outcomes. GIMI's assessment measures your organization against those requirements, identifies the gaps, and gives you a clear path to a certifiable system.

GIMI is uniquely positioned to deliver it: the first and largest organizational innovation certification body, accredited to ISO/IEC 17024 with a framework endorsed as ISO+, and our sister organization IAOIP is the US committee founder for ISO/TC 279, Innovation Management.
