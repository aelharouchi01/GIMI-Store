Overview

Stop reacting to change. Start anticipating it.

The future does not arrive all at once; it sends signals first. Future Foresight trains your people to read them and act early.

Built on the S4Futures framework with uncertainty at its core, the program develops the analytical skills to question assumptions, scan for trends and weak signals, and build rigorous scenarios, then turn that foresight into strategy and innovation decisions. It runs from Professional to Leader to Officer levels, with a trainer track to scale the capability across your organization.

The S4 Framework

Four steps from signals to strategy.

1
Scoping & Designing
Frame the foresight challenge and design the approach.
2
Scanning & Screening
Detect trends, weak signals, and drivers of change.
3
Scenarios & Foresight
Build rigorous, evidence-based scenarios under uncertainty.
4
Strategy & Innovation
Turn foresight into strategy and innovation decisions.

Levels

A pathway from practitioner to trainer.

Level 1 · Professional

Sharpen the analytical skills to question assumptions and read weak signals.

Level 2 · Leader

Master the S4Futures framework for rigorous, structured scenario work.

Level 3 · Officer

Lead a full foresight project end to end for a real organization.

Trainer Track

Become certified to train others in Future Foresight at Levels 1 and 2.
