Overview

Stop reacting to change. Start anticipating it.

The future does not arrive all at once; it sends signals first. Future Foresight trains your people to read them and act early.

Built on the S4Futures framework with uncertainty at its core, the program develops the analytical skills to question assumptions, scan for trends and weak signals, and build rigorous scenarios, then turn that foresight into strategy and innovation decisions. It runs from Professional to Leader to Officer levels, with a trainer track to scale the capability across your organization.

The S4 Framework

Four steps from signals to strategy.

1
Scoping & Designing
Frame the foresight challenge and design the approach.
2
Scanning & Screening
Detect trends, weak signals, and drivers of change.
3
Scenarios & Foresight
Build rigorous, evidence-based scenarios under uncertainty.
4
Strategy & Innovation
Turn foresight into strategy and innovation decisions.

Levels

A pathway from practitioner to trainer.

Level 1 · Professional

A sound entrance to the field, strengthening analytical and intuitive skills, fundamentals, key principles, and the main schools of thought.

  • No eligibility requirements
  • Includes: FF Body of Knowledge L1 Guide, exam fee, certificate, remote proctor
  • Optional workshop: 3 half-days, live online or on-site

Level 2 · Leader

The end-to-end Foresight process using the S4Futures framework, linking scenarios with strategy and innovation.

  • Requires Certified Foresight Professional (Level 1)
  • Includes: FF Body of Knowledge L2 Guide, exam fee, certificate, remote proctor
  • Optional workshop: 4 half-days, live online or on-site

Level 3 · Officer

Apply Levels 1 and 2 to a real organizational challenge and lead a full Foresight project, submitting a White Paper.

  • Requires Certified Foresight Leader (Level 2)
  • Includes: project guidelines, review by ALVA and the GIMI Board, Chief Foresight Officer certificate
  • Optional: 2 mentoring sessions with a seasoned expert

Trainer Track

Become certified to train others in Future Foresight, scaling the capability across your organization.

  • Certifies you to train and assess at Levels 1 and 2
  • Includes the facilitation toolkit and trainer materials
  • Requires Certified Foresight Leader (Level 2)
